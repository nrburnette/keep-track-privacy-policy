Privacy Policy for Keep Track

Effective Date: April 9, 2026

Keep Track ("the app") is designed to help users track personal goals locally on their device.

General Tips:

Create a category of something you wish to track, like protein.  Set the goal in the title of the category if you have one, example: Protein Goal 190g.  

Long press an entry to delete it.  

Long press a category to rename it or delete it.

Press the pallette icon to customize the colors of an individual category.  


Information Collection and Use:

Keep Track does not collect, store, or share any personal data on external servers. All data entered into the app (such as tracking categories and entries) is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to the developer or any third parties.

Permissions

The app does not require access to sensitive device permissions such as location, contacts, or camera.

Data Sharing

Keep Track does not share any user data with third parties.

Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected within the app listing.
